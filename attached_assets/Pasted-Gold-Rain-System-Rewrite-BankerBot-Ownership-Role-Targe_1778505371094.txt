PART 4 — BASIC GOLD RAIN BEHAVIOR
==================================================

/goldrain all 10 5

Means:
- Target group: all
- Total giveaway: 10 gold
- Winners: 5
- Reward each: 2 gold
- Eligible players must be currently in the room
- Do not pick bots
- Do not pick the same player twice

BankerBot public start message:

🌧️ Gold Rain Started!
Target: All
Total: 10g
Winners: 5
Reward: 2g each

Then public winner/tip messages:

💸 Tipped @Player1 2g gold!
💸 Tipped @Player2 2g gold!
💸 Tipped @Player3 2g gold!
💸 Tipped @Player4 2g gold!
💸 Tipped @Player5 2g gold!

Final complete summary should NOT be public.
Final complete summary should be whispered only to the tipper/staff member who started Gold Rain.

BankerBot whisper to tipper:

5 winners received 2g each.
Total: 10g
Logged: YES
Pending manual gold: 0g

PART 5 — PARTY / SLOW MODE
==================================================

Add slow/party mode so Gold Rain tips players one by one with interval delay.

Commands:

/goldrain slow <group> <total_gold> <winners> <interval_seconds>
/goldrain party <group> <total_gold> <winners> <interval_seconds>

Examples:

/goldrain slow all 50 10 15
/goldrain party subs 100 20 10

/goldrain slow all 50 10 15 means:
- Pick 10 eligible players from all room players
- Total giveaway is 50g
- Each winner gets 5g
- Tip one winner every 15 seconds

/goldrain party subs 100 20 10 means:
- Pick 20 subscribed players currently in the room
- Total giveaway is 100g
- Each winner gets 5g
- Tip one winner every 10 seconds

Public start message:

🌧️ Gold Rain Party Started!
Target: Subs
Total: 100g
Winners: 20
Reward: 5g each
Interval: 10s
Stay in the room for the giveaway!

Every interval, BankerBot publicly says:

💸 Tipped @Player1 5g gold!

Next interval:

💸 Tipped @Player2 5g gold!

Final complete summary is whispered only to the tipper:

🌧️ Gold Rain Complete!
20 winners received 5g each.
Total: 100g
Logged: YES
Pending manual gold: 0g

Do not send final complete summary publicly.
