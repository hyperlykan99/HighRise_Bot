==================================================
SEND COINS FEATURE
==================================================

Command:
- /send <username> <amount>

Rules:
- Sender cannot send to themselves.
- Amount must be a positive whole number.
- Sender must have enough coins.
- Minimum send amount: 10 coins.
- Maximum send amount per transaction: 1000 coins by default.
- Daily send limit: 3000 coins by default.
- Apply transfer tax if enabled.
- Default transfer tax: 5%.
- Tax is removed from the amount received.
- Example:
  /send Claire 100
  sender loses 100
  Claire receives 95
  tax burned = 5

Messages:
"✅ Sent 95c to @Claire. Fee: 5c."
"❌ Not enough coins."
"❌ You cannot send coins to yourself."
"❌ Transfer blocked. Check /bank."

==================================================
ANTI-ABUSE RULES
==================================================

Add transfer eligibility requirements.

Default requirements:
- Account must be seen by the bot for at least 3 days before sending.
- Player must be at least Level 3 to send.
- Player must have earned at least 500 total coins from games/daily/shop rewards before sending.
- Player must have claimed /daily at least 2 different days.
- Player must have won at least 5 mini games OR played at least 5 blackjack/coinflip rounds.
- Player cannot send more than 3000 coins per day.
- Player cannot send more than 1000 coins in one transaction.
- Player cannot send coins if bankblocked.

Receiving rules:
- New accounts can receive coins, but cannot send until eligible.
- If receiving account is suspicious/new, log the transaction as high risk.

Important:
These rules should reduce alt-account abuse but not break normal players.

==================================================
ALT ABUSE DETECTION / RISK FLAGS
==================================================

Add a risk scoring system for each transaction.

Flag a transaction as suspicious if:
- Sender account age in bot database is less than 3 days.
- Sender level is below 3.
- Sender has mostly coins from /daily and very few game wins.
- Sender sends more than 80% of their balance.
- Sender sends coins to the same receiver repeatedly.
- Multiple low-level users send coins to the same receiver.
- Sender has received coins and quickly forwards them to another user.
- Sender sends coins within 10 minutes of claiming /daily.

Risk levels:
- LOW
- MEDIUM
- HIGH

Store risk_level and risk_reason in transaction history.

If HIGH risk:
- Block the transfer by default.
- Tell player: "Transfer blocked by bank safety rules."
- Log it for staff.

If MEDIUM risk:
- Allow transfer but log it.
- Optional: reduce daily send limit.
