1. Improve exchange-rate detection

These should all route to live exchange source:

- ai exchange rate now
- ai current USD to PHP
- ai usd to php
- ai 1 usd to php
- ai how much php is 1 usd
- ai how many pesos is 1 dollar
- ai convert 1 usd to php
- ai convert 10 dollars to pesos
- ai dollar to peso rate
- ai php per usd

Entity extraction:
- source_currency: USD if user says usd, dollar, dollars
- target_currency: PHP if user says php, peso, pesos
- amount: default 1 if missing

Examples:
User: ai how much php is 1 usd
AI: 1 USD is about [rate] PHP. Rates change daily.

User: ai convert 10 dollars to pesos
AI: 10 USD is about [converted amount] PHP.

2. Improve teleport intent detection

These should route to teleport/spawn system:

- ai tele me to mod
- ai teleport me to mod
- ai tp me to mod
- ai take me to mod
- ai bring me to mod
- ai go to mod
- ai send me to mod room
- ai move me to lounge
- ai teleport to bar

Treat "tele" as shorthand for teleport.

Entity extraction:
- action: teleport_self
- target_spawn: mod / lounge / bar / casino / vip / etc.

If target spawn exists:
Run safe teleport command equivalent:
!tele mod
or existing teleport handler.

If target spawn does not exist:
Reply:
“I couldn’t find that teleport spot. Try !tele list to see available spots.”

Do not teleport other players unless staff/admin permissions allow it.
