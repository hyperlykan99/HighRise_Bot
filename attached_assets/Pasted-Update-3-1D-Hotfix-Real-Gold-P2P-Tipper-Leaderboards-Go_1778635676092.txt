Update 3.1D Hotfix — Real Gold P2P Tipper Leaderboards

Goal:
Add separate leaderboards for real player-to-player Highrise gold tips.

Important correction:
P2P means REAL GOLD tipped from one real player to another real player.

P2P does NOT mean coin transfers.
Do NOT use !send coin transactions for this leaderboard.

This leaderboard must be separate from:
- !topdonators = gold tipped to my bots/room
- !toprich = coin balance
- !send / coin transfers = coins only

Do not wipe data.
Do not change tokens.
Do not change room ID.
Do not reset balances, VIP, mining/fishing, casino, party tip, notifications, daily streaks, or existing donation data.
Do not change coin reward rates.
Do not auto-subscribe users.
Do not give coins for P2P gold tips unless an existing system already intentionally does that.

PART 2 — WHAT COUNTS AS REAL P2P GOLD TIP
==================================================

Count only real Highrise gold tip events where:

sender = real player
receiver = real player
amount = positive gold amount

Examples that COUNT:
@PlayerA tips @PlayerB 10g
@PlayerA tips @PlayerC 5g
@PlayerB tips @PlayerA 1g

Examples that DO NOT COUNT:
@Player tips BankingBot
@Player tips ChillTopiaMC
@Player tips MasterAngler
@Player tips any owned bot
bot tips player
owner !goldtip to player
Party Tip wallet tips to player
!tip all
!tip [players] [amount] from party wallet
gold rain
!send coin transfer
!pay coin transfer
blackjack/poker bets
mining/fishing rewards
daily rewards
VIP/shop purchases
admin balance adjustments

Important:
Gold tipped to bots belongs in !topdonators.
Gold tipped player-to-player belongs in !toptippers / !toptipped.

==================================================

- count as room donation

If receiver is a real player:
- count as P2P gold tip
- update !toptippers and !toptipped
- do NOT update !topdonators
- do NOT apply bot donation coin reward

If receiver is unknown:
- log as ignored/unknown
- do not count unless safely classified
