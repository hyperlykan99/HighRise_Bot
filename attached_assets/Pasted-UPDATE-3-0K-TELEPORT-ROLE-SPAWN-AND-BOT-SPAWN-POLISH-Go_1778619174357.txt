PART 9 — ROLE SPAWN SYSTEM
==================================================

Clarify behavior:
Role spawn is NOT “spawn a role whenever I want.”
Role spawn means:

When a user with a role enters the room, they automatically spawn at the saved role spawn spot.

Commands:
!setrolespawn [role] here
!rolespawn [role]
!rolespawns
!delrolespawn [role]
!autospawn on
!autospawn off
!autospawn status

Owner/Manager only for setup.

!setrolespawn [role] here:
- saves current position as spawn for that role

Success:

Auto-spawn users with this role: YES

Usage:
Usage: !setrolespawn [role] here
Example: !setrolespawn vip here

!rolespawn [role]:
- shows saved spawn for role
- does not move all users

Expected:
🏷️ Role Spawn

Status: saved
Autospawn: ON/OFF

!rolespawns:
Shows saved role spawns.

Expected:
🏷️ Role Spawns
vip — saved

!delrolespawn [role]:
Deletes role spawn.

!autospawn on/off/status:
Controls whether users are automatically moved on room entry.

Expected:

Status: ON
Saved Role Spawns: [count]

==================================================
PART 10 — ROLE SPAWN ENTRY BEHAVIOR
==================================================

On user join:
- check if autospawn is ON
- check user’s role(s)
- choose highest priority role spawn
- teleport user to that role’s saved spawn
- do not spam public chat
- optionally whisper:

🏷️ Welcome!
You were moved to your role spawn.

If teleport fails:
- log error
- do not crash
- do not repeatedly retry forever

Priority example:
Owner > Admin > Manager > Staff > VIP > Subscriber > Player

If multiple roles exist, highest priority wins.

Do not move bots using role spawn.
Bots use bot spawn.

PART 11 — BOT SPAWN SYSTEM
==================================================

Bot spawn means:
When a bot joins/rejoins/restarts, it moves to its saved bot spawn.

Commands:
!setbotspawnhere [bot]
!setbotspawn [bot] [x] [y] [z] [facing]
!botspawns
!clearbotspawn [bot]

Owner/Manager only.

!setbotspawnhere [bot]:
- saves command user’s current position as spawn for target bot
- moves target bot there now if in room
- target must be known bot or current room bot account

Success:
🤖 Bot Spawn Saved
Bot: @ChillTopiaMC
Moved Here: YES
Auto-return on rejoin: YES

Usage:
Usage: !setbotspawnhere [bot]
Example: !setbotspawnhere @ChillTopiaMC

If bot not found:
⚠️ Bot not found in room.

If cannot read owner position:
⚠️ I could not read your position. Move slightly and try again.

!setbotspawn [bot] [x] [y] [z] [facing]:
Saves bot spawn from coordinates.

Usage:
Usage: !setbotspawn [bot] [x] [y] [z] [facing]
Example: !setbotspawn @ChillTopiaMC 10 0 5 FrontLeft

!botspawns:
Shows saved bot spawns.

Expected:

@BankingBot — saved
@BlackJackBot — not set

!clearbotspawn [bot]:

Expected:
