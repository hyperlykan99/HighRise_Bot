PART 12 — NATURAL BLACKJACK PAYOUT FIX
==================================================

Fix fairness issue:
Getting a natural blackjack should actually pay 2.5x total return.

Definition:
Natural Blackjack = first 2 cards are:
- Ace + 10-value card
- total = 21
- exactly 2 cards

Natural Blackjack payout:
- total return = bet × 2.5
- profit = bet × 1.5

Example:
Bet: 100c
Natural Blackjack total payout: 250c
Net profit: 150c

Important:
Regular 21 after hitting is NOT natural blackjack.

Example:
7 + 4 + K = 21
This is a normal win, not blackjack bonus.
Normal win total return = bet × 2.
Net profit = bet.

Rules:
- fix payout if natural blackjack currently only pays normal win
- do not change normal win payout
- do not change push refund
- do not change bust loss
- do not duplicate payout after restart
- record payout reason clearly

PART 14 — BONUS SYSTEM
==================================================

Implement a simple blackjack bonus system.

Bonuses should be clear, controlled, and not break economy.

Recommended bonuses:

1. Natural Blackjack Bonus
- Required.
- 2.5x total payout.
- This is the main blackjack bonus.

2. Perfect 21 Bonus
- Optional but requested as part of bonus system.
- If player reaches exactly 21 after hitting, give a small bonus.
- Suggested bonus: +10% of bet.
- Example: bet 100c, normal 21 win pays 200c total, bonus adds 10c.
- Total paid: 210c.
- Do NOT make this 2.5x.

3. 5-Card Charlie Bonus
- If player reaches 5 cards without busting, player auto-wins.
- Suggested payout: normal win + 25% bet bonus.
- Example: bet 100c, total paid 225c.
- Only if current blackjack rules can support it safely.

4. Suited Natural Blackjack Bonus
- If natural blackjack cards are same suit.
- Suggested bonus: extra +25% of bet on top of natural blackjack payout.
- Example: bet 100c, natural BJ pays 250c, suited bonus adds 25c, total 275c.

5. Pair Start Bonus
- Optional small bonus if first two cards are same rank.
- Suggested bonus: +5% of bet.
- Only paid if player does not bust and final result is not loss.
- If risky, skip this one.

Use conservative bonuses.
Do not create huge payouts.
Do not pay bonus twice.
