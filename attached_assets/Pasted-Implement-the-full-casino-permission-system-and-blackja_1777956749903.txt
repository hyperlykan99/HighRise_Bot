PART 3: CASUAL BLACKJACK /bj
==================================================

Add casual lobby blackjack.

Commands:
- /bj join <bet>
- /bj leave
- /bj players
- /bj table
- /bj hit
- /bj stand
- /bj double
- /bj rules
- /bj stats
- /bj cancel
- /bj settings

Permissions:
- /bj cancel is owner/admin/manager only.
- /bj settings is owner/admin/manager only.
- Other /bj commands are public.

Default casual blackjack settings:
- min_bet: 10
- max_bet: 1000
- win_payout: 2.0
- blackjack_payout: 2.5
- push_rule: refund
- dealer_hits_soft_17: true
- lobby_countdown: 15 seconds
- turn_timer: 30 seconds
- max_players: 6

Lobby rules:
- When first player uses /bj join <bet>, open BJ lobby.
- Start a 15-second countdown.
- Other players can join during countdown.
- Minimum players: 1.
- Maximum players: 6.
- One casual blackjack lobby/table active at a time.
- Validate bet amount.
- Player must have enough coins.
- Deduct bet when player joins.
- If player uses /bj leave before round starts, refund bet.
- Once round starts, no more players can join.
- If /bj cancel is used, cancel lobby/round and refund active bets.

Round rules:
- Casual BJ can create a fresh shuffled deck each round.
- Deal 2 cards to each player.
- Deal 2 cards to dealer.
- Show only 1 dealer card at first.
- Players take turns one at a time.
- Player can /bj hit, /bj stand, or /bj double.
- /bj double requires enough coins to match the bet.
- If player doubles:
  deduct extra bet,
  double total bet,
  deal exactly 1 card,
  auto-stand.
- If player busts, move to next player.
- If player gets blackjack on first 2 cards, mark blackjack.
- After all players finish, dealer reveals hidden card.
- Dealer hits until 17 or higher.
- Dealer hits soft 17 if setting is true.
- Compare each player against dealer.
- Pay winners.
- Refund pushes.
- Losses keep deducted bet.

Payouts:
- Normal win pays 2.0x total bet.
- Blackjack pays 2.5x total bet.
- Push refunds total bet.
- Loss pays nothing.
- Bet is deducted when joining.

Card rules:
- 2-10 = face value
- J, Q, K = 10
- A = 11 or 1
- Blackjack = Ace + 10-value card on first 2 cards only

Timer rules:
- Lobby countdown is 15 seconds.
- Each player turn is 30 seconds.
- If player does not act, auto-stand.
- Use asyncio.create_task for countdown and turn timers.
- Do not block the bot.
- Cancel timers safely when game ends or player acts.

Messages:

"↩️ @username left BJ. Bet refunded."
