Current status:
- modules/tips.py now adds Luxe Tickets, but it still uses get_luxe_rate().
- It still uses old min_tip_gold and daily_cap_gold logic.
- modules/luxe_admin.py exists, but !addtickets, !settickets, !ticketbalance, and !ticketadmin may not be routed in main.py.

Goal:
Make tips exactly:
1 Highrise gold = 1 🎫 Luxe Ticket

No minimum.
No daily cap.
No ChillCoins.
No multiplier/rate.

daily_cap_gold

Replace with:

Then:
from modules.luxe import add_luxe_balance, log_luxe_transaction
