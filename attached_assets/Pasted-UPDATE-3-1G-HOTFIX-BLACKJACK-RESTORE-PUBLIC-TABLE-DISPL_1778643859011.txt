PART 3 — SHOE / DECK RESTORE RULE
==================================================

The shoe/deck is the source of fairness.

On bot restart:
- load saved blackjack table
- load saved shoe/deck exactly as it was
- do not create a new shoe
- do not reshuffle
- do not reset deck
- do not redraw dealer or player cards

Only create a new shoe when:
- no active blackjack round exists
- previous round fully ended
- shoe is naturally exhausted and reshuffle is intended by existing logic

If active round exists and saved shoe/deck is missing/corrupt:
- do NOT continue the round
- refund all active bets
- cancel round safely
- message:

⚠️ Blackjack round restore failed.
Shoe data was missing. Bets refunded.
Start again with !bet [amount].

Do not let blackjack continue with a fresh shoe in the middle of a round.

PART 12 — NATURAL BLACKJACK PAYOUT FIX
==================================================

Fix fairness bug:
Natural blackjack should pay correctly.

Definition:
Natural blackjack = first 2 cards are:
- Ace + any 10-value card
- total = 21
- exactly 2 cards

Natural blackjack payout:
- total return = 2.5x bet
- profit = 1.5x bet

Example:
Bet 10,000c
Natural blackjack total return = 25,000c
Net profit = 15,000c

Important:
Regular 21 after hitting is NOT natural blackjack.
Regular 21 pays normal win unless bonus rules below apply.

Do not pay natural blackjack bonus for:
- 3-card 21
- 4-card 21
- 5-card 21
- split hands unless existing rules allow and explicitly safe

If current system uses profit-only accounting:
- credit profit 1.5x bet plus original bet already held/refunded depending current balance model
- avoid double-credit

If current system uses total-return accounting:
- return 2.5x bet total

Make payout accounting consistent with existing blackjack balance model.

PART 13 — BONUS SYSTEM
==================================================

Implement simple blackjack bonus system.

Bonuses should be clear, limited, and safe.

Bonus types:

1. Natural Blackjack Bonus
- Requirement: first 2 cards = Ace + 10-value
- Payout: 2.5x total return
- Message:
  🃏 BLACKJACK! @Player wins 2.5x.

2. 5-Card Charlie
- Requirement: player has 5 cards and has not busted
- Payout: normal win + 25% bonus profit
- Example:
  Bet 100c
  Normal win total return = 200c
  5-card bonus adds 25c profit
  Total return = 225c
- Message:
  🎉 5-Card Charlie! @Player wins bonus.

3. Perfect 21 Bonus
- Requirement: player reaches exactly 21 with 3+ cards
- Payout: normal win + 10% bonus profit
- Message:
  🎯 Perfect 21 bonus!

4. Suited Natural Blackjack Bonus
- Requirement: natural blackjack and both cards same suit
- Payout: 3x total return instead of 2.5x
- Message:
  💎 Suited Blackjack! @Player wins 3x.

Rules:
- Bonuses must not stack in abusive ways.
- Highest applicable bonus wins.
- Natural blackjack beats Perfect 21.
- Suited natural blackjack beats natural blackjack.
- 5-Card Charlie only if not busted and no natural blackjack.
- Bonus applies once per hand.
- No duplicate bonus after restart.
- Save bonus_applied flag per player hand.

If this is too much to safely implement at once:
Priority:
1. Natural Blackjack 2.5x
2. Suited Blackjack 3x
3. 5-Card Charlie
4. Perfect 21
