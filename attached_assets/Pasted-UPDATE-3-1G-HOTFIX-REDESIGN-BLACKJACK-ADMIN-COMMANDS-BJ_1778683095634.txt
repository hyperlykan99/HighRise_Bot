PART 21 — DEALING LOGIC MUST USE TEST BEFORE NORMAL DRAW
==================================================

Fix current bad behavior:
Normal cards are dealt first, then forced cards overwrite them.

Correct behavior:
At deal time, choose source first.

For each needed card:
- if a bjtest forced card exists:
  - fake mode: use fake card, do not touch shoe
  - payout mode: use fake card, do not touch shoe
  - shoe mode: remove exact card from shoe
- else:
  - draw normal card from shoe

Do not:
- draw normal card then overwrite it
- remove normal card from shoe then replace hand with fake card
- duplicate cards in shoe mode
- corrupt shoe count
