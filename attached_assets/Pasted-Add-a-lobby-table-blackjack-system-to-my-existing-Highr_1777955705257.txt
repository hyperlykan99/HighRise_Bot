Goal:
Blackjack should be lobby-style. Players join a table, a countdown starts, then the round begins for everyone who joined.

Commands:
- /bj join <bet>
- /bj leave
- /bj players
- /bj table
- /bj hit
- /bj stand
- /bj double
- /bj rules
- /bj stats
- /bj cancel admin only
- /bj settings admin only

Lobby rules:
- When the first player types /bj join <bet>, open a blackjack lobby.
- Start a 60-second countdown.
- Other players can join during the countdown.
- Minimum players: 1.
- Maximum players: 6.
- One blackjack lobby/table active at a time.
- Validate the bet before joining.
- Minimum bet default: 10 coins.
- Maximum bet default: 1000 coins.
- Player must have enough coins to join.
- Deduct the bet when the player joins.
- If the player uses /bj leave before the round starts, refund their bet.
- Once the round starts, no more players can join.
- If admin uses /bj cancel, cancel the lobby/round and refund all active bets.

Round rules:
- Deal 2 cards to each player.
- Deal 2 cards to the dealer.
- Show only 1 dealer card at first.
- Players take turns one at a time.
- On their turn, a player can use:
  /bj hit
  /bj stand
  /bj double
- /bj double requires the player to have enough coins to match the bet.
- If player doubles:
  deduct the extra bet,
  double their total bet,
  give exactly 1 card,
  then auto-stand.
- If player busts, move to the next player.
- If player gets blackjack on the first 2 cards, mark it as blackjack.
- After all players finish, dealer reveals hidden card.
- Dealer hits until 17 or higher.
- Compare each player against dealer.
- Pay winners.
- Refund pushes.
- Losses keep the deducted bet.

Payouts:
- Normal win pays 2.0x total bet.
- Blackjack pays 2.5x total bet.
- Push refunds the total bet.
- Loss pays nothing.
- Bet is already deducted when joining.
- Example:
  Bet 100, lose = 0 returned.
  Bet 100, win = 200 returned.
  Bet 100, push = 100 returned.
  Bet 100, blackjack = 250 returned.

Card rules:
- 2-10 = face value.
- J, Q, K = 10.
- A = 11 or 1, whichever is best without busting.
- Blackjack means A + 10-value card on the first 2 cards only.

Turn timer:
- Each player has 30 seconds to act.
- If the player does not act, auto-stand.
- Use asyncio.create_task for lobby countdown and turn timers.
- Do not block the whole bot while timers run.
- Cancel timers safely when:
  lobby is cancelled,
  round ends,
  player acts,
  bot restarts.
