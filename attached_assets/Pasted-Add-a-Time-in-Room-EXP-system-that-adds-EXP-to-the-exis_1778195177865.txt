==================================================
PART 1 — CORE TIME-IN-ROOM EXP SYSTEM
==================================================

Players earn existing profile EXP based on time spent in the room.

Rules:
1. Connected players earn EXP every minute.
2. AFK/idle players still earn EXP.
3. Players earn more EXP per minute the longer they stay in the room.
4. Active players can get a small bonus.
5. EXP must be added to the existing player EXP field/system.
6. Existing level calculation must be used.
7. Existing profile should show the updated EXP/level.
8. Only Host/EmceeBot should award time EXP.
9. Prevent duplicate EXP from multiple bots.
10. Do not backpay offline time.
11. Do not award disconnected users.
12. Do not award bot accounts unless staff enables bot EXP.

Do NOT use negative AFK wording.
Do NOT say:
- EXP paused because AFK
- AFK blocked
- Move or chat to earn EXP
- Kick AFK

Use positive wording:
- Stay in the room to earn EXP.
- Activity gives a small bonus.
- Longer stay gives better EXP rate.

==================================================
PART 2 — EXP COMPUTATION
==================================================

Use scaled EXP tiers based on continuous time present in the room.

Default tiers:
- 0 to 30 minutes: 1 EXP/min
- 30 to 60 minutes: 1.25 EXP/min
- 1 to 2 hours: 1.5 EXP/min
- 2 to 4 hours: 2 EXP/min
- 4 to 8 hours: 2.5 EXP/min
- 8+ hours: 3 EXP/min

Optional active bonus:
- Active recently: +0.25 EXP/min
- Active bonus window: 5 minutes
- AFK/idle players still earn the tier rate without bonus.

Daily cap:
- Default daily_time_exp_cap = 1500 EXP
- Once player reaches the daily cap, stop awarding time EXP until next daily reset.
- Do not remove players from the room.
- Do not punish players.
- Just stop adding more time EXP for that day.

Tick:
- Default tick_seconds = 60

Per tick computation:
1. Find users currently present in the room.
2. For each real player, calculate how long they have continuously stayed in the room/current session.
3. Determine tier EXP per minute based on stay duration.
4. Calculate base EXP for the tick:
   base_exp = tier_exp_per_minute * (elapsed_seconds_since_last_award / 60)
5. If player was active within active_bonus_window_minutes:
   bonus_exp = active_bonus_per_minute * (elapsed_seconds_since_last_award / 60)
6. total_exp = base_exp + bonus_exp
7. Apply daily_time_exp_cap.
8. Add total_exp to the existing player EXP/level system.
9. Recalculate level using existing level function/system.
10. Save last_award_time.

Example:
- Player at 20 minutes in room earns 1 EXP this minute.
- Player at 90 minutes in room earns 1.5 EXP this minute.
- Player at 5 hours in room earns 2.5 EXP this minute.
- Player at 9 hours in room earns 3 EXP this minute.
- If daily cap reached, award 0 until daily reset.
