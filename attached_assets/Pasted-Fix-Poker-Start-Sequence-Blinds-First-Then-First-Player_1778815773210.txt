Fix Poker Start Sequence — Blinds First, Then First Player Cards, Then Turn Announcement

Current problems:
1. First player after round starts still does not receive their cards.
2. Other players still do not see whose turn it is.

Goal:
When a poker hand starts, follow a strict sequence:

1. Dealer opens/starts round.
2. Dealer announces blinds.
3. Wait 5 seconds.
4. First player whose turn it is receives their private cards by whisper.
5. All other players see:
   ⏳ @FirstPlayer’s turn.
6. Current player sees:
   🎯 Your turn. Cards: [card1] [card2]
7. Do not allow actions before the first player card whisper is sent.

Do not change:
- blackjack
- jail
- luxe tickets
- tips
- economy
- workflows

Part 1 — Strict hand start sequence

Find poker hand start function:
- start_hand
- begin_hand
- open_table
- start_round
- deal_hole_cards
- announce_blinds
- set_current_turn

Change the flow to:

async def start_poker_hand(...):
    deal hole cards to all active players
    save cards to state/database

    announce:
    🃏 Poker hand started.
    Blinds: SB [amount] / BB [amount]

    await asyncio.sleep(5)

    set current_turn_user_id to first actor

    await send_first_turn_cards_and_announce(bot, table)

Important:
Do not start turn timer until after send_first_turn_cards_and_announce finishes.

Part 2 — New helper

Add helper:

async def send_first_turn_cards_and_announce(bot, table):
    current_id = table.current_turn_user_id
    current_player = get_player_by_id(table, current_id)

    cards = get_hole_cards(table, current_id)

    if cards exist:
        whisper current player:
        🎯 Your turn. Cards: [card1] [card2]
        Use !check, !call, !raise [amount], or !fold.
    else:
        whisper current player:
        🎯 Your turn, but your cards were not found. Type !cards or ask staff.
        print error.

    public chat:
    ⏳ @CurrentPlayer’s turn.

This public message lets everyone else know whose turn it is.

Part 3 — Send all player cards safely too

Still send cards to everyone when the hand starts, but the first actor gets an extra guaranteed whisper after 5 seconds.

Flow:
1. Deal cards.
2. Try to whisper all players their cards.
3. Announce blinds.
4. Wait 5 seconds.
5. Guaranteed resend to first actor.
6. Announce first actor turn publicly.

This fixes first player missing cards.

Part 4 — Turn change announcement

Every time current_turn_user_id changes, call:

await announce_poker_turn(bot, table)

Behavior:
- Whisper current player their cards again:
  🎯 Your turn. Cards: [card1] [card2]
- Public chat:
  ⏳ @Player’s turn.

If current bet exists:
Public chat:
⏳ @Player’s turn. Call [amount] 🪙, raise, or fold.

Part 6 — Prevent action before first turn ready

Add flag:
table.first_turn_ready = False
