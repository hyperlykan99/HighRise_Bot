Realistic lobby rules:

- Admin /rbj cancel refunds active bets and cancels the lobby or round.

Realistic round rules:
- Deal cards from the shared shoe only.
- Deal 2 cards to each player.
- Deal 2 cards to dealer.
- Show only 1 dealer card at first.
- Players take turns.
- Player commands during turn:
  /rbj hit
  /rbj stand
  /rbj double
- /rbj double requires enough coins to match the bet.
- If player doubles:
  deduct extra bet,
  double total bet,
  deal exactly one card,
  auto-stand.
- If player busts, move to next player.
- After all players finish, dealer reveals hidden card.
- Dealer hits until 17 or higher.
- Dealer hits soft 17 if the setting is enabled.
- Compare each player against dealer.

Payouts:
- Normal win pays 2.0x total bet.
- Blackjack pays 2.5x total bet.

- Loss pays nothing.
- Bet is already deducted when joining.

Card rules:
- 2-10 = face value.
- J, Q, K = 10.
- A = 11 or 1.
- Blackjack means Ace + 10-value card on the first 2 cards only.
