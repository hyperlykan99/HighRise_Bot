==================================================
PART 5 — ROLESPAWN HANDLER REQUIREMENTS
==================================================

!rolespawn [role]
Shows saved automatic entry spawn for that role.

!rolespawns
Shows all role spawn positions.

!setrolespawn [role] here
Saves current position as automatic entry spawn for that role.

!delrolespawn [role]
Deletes saved role spawn.

!autospawn on/off/status
Controls automatic teleport on room entry.

!autospawn debug [user]
Shows role detection and selected role spawn.

Important:
RoleSpawn means:
When a user enters the room, they are automatically teleported to the saved spawn for their role.
