Redesign the Badge System into an Emoji Badge Market and Player Marketplace.

GOAL:
I want badges to be emoji-based.
Players can buy emoji badges from the bot shop.
Only some emojis should be purchasable directly depending on quality/rarity.
Players can sell their owned emoji badges to other players on a player market and choose their own price.

IMPORTANT:
- Do not remove existing features.
- Keep coins, bank, profiles, titles, VIP, casino, BJ, RBJ, poker, events, notifications, staff roles, and admin tools.
- Use SQLite.
- Keep all Highrise messages under 250 characters.
- Split long shop/market pages.
- Prevent crashes from bad inputs.
- Add explicit command routing before unknown-command fallback.
- Badge display format should stay:
Emoji > Title > Username

Example:
"💎 [Elite] @Marion"

PART 4: BADGE SHOP COMMANDS
==================================================

Player commands:
- /shop badges
- /shop badges <page>
- /badgeinfo <badge_id>
- /buy badge <badge_id>
- /equip badge <badge_id>
- /unequip badge
- /mybadges
- /badges
- /badges <username>

Behavior:
/shop badges
Shows purchasable badges only.
Paginate.

Example:
"🏷️ Badges 1/5
star ⭐ 500c
fire 🔥 2,500c
diamond 💎 10,000c
More: /shop badges 2"

Keep each message under 250 characters.

/badgeinfo <badge_id>
Shows:
"💎 diamond | Rare | 10,000c | Tradeable YES"

/buy badge <badge_id>
- Check badge exists.
- Check purchasable = true.
- Check player has enough coins.
- Prevent duplicate purchase.
- Deduct coins.
- Add badge to user_badges.
- Message:
"✅ Bought badge 💎 diamond."

/equip badge <badge_id>
- Player must own badge.
- Unequip old badge.
- Equip new badge.
- Message:
"✅ Equipped badge 💎."

/unequip badge
- Removes equipped badge.
- Message:
"✅ Badge unequipped."

/mybadges
Shows owned badges:
"Your badges: ⭐ 💎 🔥. Equip: /equip badge diamond"

/badges <username>
Shows public owned/equipped badges if inventory privacy allows.

==================================================
PART 5: PLAYER BADGE MARKETPLACE
==================================================

Players can sell owned emoji badges to other players.

Player market commands:
- /badgemarket
- /badgemarket <page>
- /badgelist <badge_id> <price>
- /badgebuy <listing_id>
- /badgecancel <listing_id>
- /mybadgelistings
- /badgeprices <badge_id>

Behavior:
/badgemarket
Shows active player listings.

Example:
"🏷️ Badge Market 1/3
#12 💎 diamond 25,000c @Seller
#13 🔥 fire 8,000c @Seller"

/badgelist <badge_id> <price>
- Player must own badge.
- Badge must be tradeable and sellable.
- Badge must not be locked.
- If currently equipped, unequip it first or block listing:
"Unequip badge before listing."
- Remove badge from active use while listed.
- Price must be positive.
- Optional price minimum: 100c.
- Optional max listing price: 1,000,000,000c.
- Message:
"✅ Listed 💎 diamond for 25,000c."

/badgebuy <listing_id>
- Buyer pays seller’s chosen price.
- Buyer must have enough coins.
- Buyer cannot buy their own listing.
- Transfer badge to buyer.
- Transfer coins to seller.
- Mark listing sold.
- Log transaction.
- Message:
"✅ Bought 💎 for 25,000c."

Seller notification:
If seller is in room or subscribed:
"🏷️ Your 💎 badge sold for 25,000c."

/badgecancel <listing_id>
- Only seller or owner/admin can cancel.
- Return badge to seller.
- Mark listing cancelled.
- Message:
"✅ Listing cancelled."

/mybadgelistings
Shows active listings by user.

/badgeprices <badge_id>
Shows recent sale prices if available:
"💎 diamond recent: 20K, 25K, 30K"

PART 6: MARKET FEES AND TAX
==================================================

Add marketplace fee setting.

Default:
- badge_market_fee_percent = 5

Commands:
- /setbadgemarketfee <percent>
Owner/admin only.

Rules:
- When badge sells, seller receives price minus fee.
- Fee is removed from economy or sent to bot treasury if treasury exists.
- Buyer pays full listed price.

Example:
Listing price: 10,000c
Fee: 5%
Seller receives: 9,500c

Messages:
"Sold for 10,000c. Fee 500c. You received 9,500c."

==================================================
PART 10: SAFETY AND ABUSE PREVENTION
==================================================

Rules:
- A listed badge cannot be equipped.
- A listed badge cannot be listed twice.
- A sold listing cannot be bought again.
- Buyer cannot buy their own listing.
- Players cannot sell locked badges.
- Staff/VIP/exclusive badges are locked by default unless admin allows.
- All market purchases must use SQLite transaction:
  1. check listing active
  2. check buyer balance
  3. deduct buyer coins
  4. credit seller coins minus fee
  5. transfer badge
  6. mark listing sold
- If any step fails, rollback transaction.
