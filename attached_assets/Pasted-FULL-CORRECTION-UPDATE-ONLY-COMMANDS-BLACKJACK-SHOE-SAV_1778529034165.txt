PART 6 — FIX ROLESPAWN MEANING
==================================================

Current issue:
RoleSpawn was misunderstood.

RoleSpawn should NOT mean:
"Spawn a role there whenever I manually command it."

Correct meaning:
RoleSpawn is the automatic entry spawn position for users with that role.

When a user enters the room:
1. Bot detects the user joined.
2. Bot checks their role.
3. Bot chooses the highest priority saved role spawn.
4. Bot teleports them there automatically.
5. If no role spawn is saved, do nothing.

Example:

!setrolespawn vip here

Means:
Every VIP who enters the room automatically spawns/teleports to the VIP spot.

Example:

!setrolespawn staff here

Means:
Every staff member who enters the room automatically spawns/teleports to the staff spot.
