==================================================
PART 5 — 1/1 BADGE RULE
==================================================

Each shop badge is 1/1.

Meaning:
- Only one original copy of each emoji badge can be bought from the badge shop.
- Once a player buys it, the shop marks it SOLD.
- The buyer becomes the only original owner.
- That owner can keep it, equip it, or sell it in player market.
- No other regular player can buy the same badge from the shop again.

Example:
Player buys 👑 Crown Badge.
Shop now shows:
👑 Crown Badge — SOLD

Rules:
- Do not create duplicate shop originals.
- Do not let two users buy the same 1/1 badge.
- Purchase must be atomic/safe.
- If two users try at the same time, only one succeeds.
- Staff duplicates do not affect public 1/1 shop ownership because staff duplicates are bound and not sellable.
