PART 4 — FIRST HUNT / FIRST FIND RACE SYSTEM
==================================================

This should be an event race, not a literal first-ever discovery.

Concept:
- Staff starts a temporary race.
- Players mine/fish during the event.
- First X players to get target rarity/item win.
- Race ends when winners are filled or timer expires.
- Old inventory does not count.
- Only new mining/fishing actions during active race count.

13E — SEND RULES
==================================================

When /subnotify <category> <message> is used:

1. Validate category.
2. Find subscribed players.
3. Filter only players who opted into that category.
4. Skip players with /notifall off.
5. Send DM/inbox message where supported.
6. Log sent, failed, skipped, and no_conversation counts.
7. Reply with summary.

Example:

EmceeBot:
🔔 Subscriber Notification Sent
Category: Events
Sent: 18
Skipped: 7
No conversation: 4
Failed: 1

If no eligible players:

EmceeBot:

No subscribed players are opted into Events.
