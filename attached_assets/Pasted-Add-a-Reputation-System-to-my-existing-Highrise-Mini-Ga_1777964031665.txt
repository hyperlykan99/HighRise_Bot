Add a Reputation System to my existing Highrise Mini Game Bot.

IMPORTANT:
- Do not remove existing features.
- Keep coins, XP, shop, bank, blackjack, quests, events, reports, staff roles, and profiles.
- Use existing SQLite database.
- Use get_display_name(username).
- Keep every Highrise message under 250 characters.
- Prevent bad inputs from crashing the bot.

Goal:
Players can give reputation points to other players once per day. Reputation is social status, not currency.

Commands:
- /rep <username>
- /reputation
- /repstats
- /toprep
- /repleaderboard

Rules:
- Players can give 1 rep to another player once every 24 hours.
- Players cannot rep themselves.
- Rep cannot be sent like coins.
- Rep cannot be bought.
- Rep cannot be transferred.
- Rep is permanent unless staff removes it.
- Use cooldowns to prevent spam.

Player messages:
"/rep Claire" gives Claire +1 reputation.
Reply:
"⭐ You gave +1 rep to @Claire."

Receiver notification:
If private message is supported:
"⭐ @Marion gave you +1 reputation!"

/reputation or /repstats shows:
- Total reputation
- Rep given
- Rep received
- Daily rep status
- Social rank

/toprep shows top 10 reputation players.

Social ranks:
- New Face: 0 rep
- Friendly: 10 rep
- Known: 25 rep
- Popular: 50 rep
- Loved: 100 rep
- Icon: 250 rep
- Celebrity: 500 rep
- Legend: 1000 rep

Database:
Create reputation table:
- username
- rep_received
- rep_given
- last_rep_given_at

Create reputation_logs table:
- id
- timestamp
- giver_username
- receiver_username
- amount
- reason

Staff commands:
- /addrep <username> <amount> admin/owner only
- /removerep <username> <amount> admin/owner only
- /replog <username> staff only

Permissions:
- Moderators/managers/admins/owners can use /replog.
- Only admin/owner can manually add/remove rep.

Anti-abuse:
- Do not allow self-rep.
- Do not allow repeated rep farming from the same account.
- Flag if many new/low-level accounts rep the same user.
- Add risk notes to reputation logs if sender is new or below level 3.

Update /profile:
Show reputation and social rank.

Update /profilehelp:
Add:
/rep <user>
/reputation
/toprep
