PART 5 — FORCE DROP BEHAVIOR
==================================================

/forcedropfish <username> <rarity>:
- stores a pending forced fishing drop by rarity
- next manual /fish or AutoFish attempt consumes it
- gives random fish from that rarity
- clears after one use

/forcedropfishitem <username> <fish name>:
- stores a pending forced fishing drop by exact fish
- next manual /fish or AutoFish attempt consumes it
- gives exact fish
- clears after one use

Rules:
- Owner only
- Secret from players
- Works with manual /fish
- Works with AutoFish
- One use only
- Clears after successful forced catch
- Does not reveal “forced” in public or player message
- Weight rolls normally
- Value calculates normally
- FXP applies normally
- Big Catch announcement triggers naturally if rarity qualifies
